  SPS Academic Research

  The society of physics students of Sun Yat-sen University (SPS-SYSU) holds an academic research every year, which aims to help freshmen in physics department quickly understand the research directions of the School of Physics of Sun Yat-sen University, and prepare for their participation in undergraduate research training.

  In 2022, I participated in academic research as a member of SPS. I cooperated with my team members (mainly Zhang Chaoyi and Xiao Wenhao) and completed the research on the topic of “Topological Quantum Computing” under the designation of Senior A, and won the second prize in the final report.

  Our final report can be viewed . I mainly finished the first part (introduction) and the fourth part (anyon and quantum weaving) of the slide. The following are the relevant reports of this research on the WeChat Official Accounts of the school.
