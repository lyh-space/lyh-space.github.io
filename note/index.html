Notes on Equilibrium Statistical Physics

This note is based on the course "Thermodynamics and Statistical Physics" taught by Prof. Fan Zhong and Prof. Haiping Huang at Sun Yat-sen University in the fall semester of 2023. This note covers only ensemble theory, mean field theory, virial expansion (using equations of state for real gases as examples), and stochastic thermodynamics, and does not cover thermodynamics or the classical statistical mechanics of nearly independent subsystems in the usual course. The notes are written in Chinese.

A pdf version of the notes can be downloaded here.

The note contains some pictures of the mean field theory of the Ising model, whose calculation and drawing code can be found in the github repository https://github.com/lyh-space/Ising-model.
